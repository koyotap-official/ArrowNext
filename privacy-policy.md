# Arrow Next Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KoyoTap handles information for Arrow Next, a mobile puzzle game. It applies to the production app distributed through app stores and to the public privacy policy URL used in store listings.

For privacy questions or support, contact: koyotap.official@gmail.com

## Summary

- Arrow Next stores gameplay progress, settings, and purchase entitlement state on your device.
- The app may use third-party services for ads, in-app purchases, app analytics, diagnostics, store distribution, and support.
- We do not require you to create an account to play the game.
- We do not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera data, or health data.
- Store and third-party SDK privacy disclosures must stay consistent with this policy before each public release.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores local game data so the game can work normally. This may include:

- Current level and unlocked level progress.
- Cleared level records.
- Tutorial and onboarding state.
- Sound, haptic, and other settings.
- Local ad-removal entitlement, ad counters, rewarded-ad state, and achievement-style progress used by game logic.

This data is stored on your device. Unless a future release clearly introduces account or cloud-save features, Arrow Next does not upload local save data to a KoyoTap account service and does not provide cross-device synchronization.

Deleting the app, clearing app data, or using an in-app reset feature may delete this local progress and settings data.

## Advertising

Arrow Next may show ads, including rewarded ads and other mobile ad formats. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob or another ad network used in the production build.

Ad providers may collect or receive information such as:

- Advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers.
- IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, device type, operating system, app version, and language or region settings.
- Ad impressions, clicks, rewards, ad errors, and fraud-prevention signals.

This information may be used to serve ads, limit ad frequency, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, comply with legal obligations, and, where permitted, personalize ads. Your choices may include device-level advertising ID controls, platform tracking permissions, consent prompts shown in the app where required, and settings offered by the ad provider.

For information about how Google uses data in apps that use Google services, see:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## In-App Purchases

Arrow Next may offer in-app purchases, such as removing ads. Purchases are processed by the app store platform, such as Apple App Store or Google Play.

KoyoTap does not receive your full payment card number or store account password. The app may receive purchase status, product identifiers, transaction or receipt information, and restore status so it can unlock purchased features, restore purchases, prevent fraud, and provide support.

Store platforms process purchase and billing information under their own privacy policies and account terms.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

Arrow Next may use analytics and diagnostic services, such as Firebase Analytics, platform analytics, crash reports, or similar tools, to understand app performance and improve gameplay quality.

These services may process information such as:

- App launches, session information, gameplay events, level progress, ad and purchase flow events, and feature usage.
- App version, device model, operating system version, language or region settings, and performance or crash diagnostics.
- Anonymous or pseudonymous identifiers used to distinguish app installations or events.

We use this information to improve app stability, balance gameplay, understand feature performance, detect errors, and support release operations. We do not use analytics events to collect your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera data, or health data.

## Support Communications

If you email support, we may receive your email address, the message you send, attachments you choose to include, and related issue details. We use this information to respond to your request, investigate issues, keep support records, and improve the app.

Do not include sensitive personal information in support emails unless it is necessary for your request.

## Third-Party Services

Arrow Next may use or interact with the following categories of third-party services:

- App store platforms, such as Apple App Store and Google Play, for distribution, purchases, reviews, and platform reports.
- Advertising services, such as Google AdMob, for ad serving, measurement, and fraud prevention.
- Analytics and diagnostic services, such as Firebase Analytics or platform diagnostics, for app performance and quality improvement.
- Email and support tools used to receive and respond to support requests.

Third-party services process information under their own privacy policies. Their practices may vary by platform, region, account settings, consent choices, and app version.

## Children's Privacy

Arrow Next is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request or another channel, contact koyotap.official@gmail.com so we can review and, where appropriate, delete the information.

If the app is configured for child-directed treatment in a particular store, region, or release, ad and analytics behavior must be configured to comply with applicable child privacy requirements before that release is published.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the app, clear app data, or use an available reset feature.

Support emails are kept only as long as reasonably needed for support, record keeping, issue follow-up, legal compliance, and abuse prevention. You may contact koyotap.official@gmail.com to request deletion of support email records associated with your request, subject to any legal or operational need to retain limited records.

Ad, analytics, diagnostics, purchase, and store records may be retained by the relevant third-party providers according to their own policies and your account or device settings. You can also use platform controls such as app permissions, advertising ID reset or deletion, tracking settings, and store account privacy settings where available.

Because Arrow Next does not currently require a KoyoTap account, there is no KoyoTap account profile to delete from within the app.

## Your Choices

Depending on your platform and region, you may be able to:

- Change device advertising ID or tracking settings.
- Respond to consent prompts shown in the app where required.
- Disable or limit personalized ads through platform or provider settings.
- Delete local app data by uninstalling the app, clearing app data, or using an available reset feature.
- Contact support to request deletion of support communications associated with your email address.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled by the app and support process. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and third-party platforms and providers are responsible for security of the services they operate.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, SDKs, store requirements, or data practices change. The latest version will be posted at the public privacy policy URL used by the app store listing.

Material changes should be reflected in the app store privacy disclosures and, where required, in the app before the updated release is published.
